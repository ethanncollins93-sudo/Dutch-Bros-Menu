Complete Guide to the Dutch Bros Menu

Dutch Bros Coffee has become one of the most popular drive-thru coffee brands in the United States, known for its energetic baristas, customizable drinks, and wide variety of flavors. Whether you are new to Dutch Bros or a regular customer, understanding the full menu can help you discover new favorites and order with confidence.

Overview of the Dutch Bros Menu

The Dutch Bros menu is designed to appeal to a wide range of tastes. It includes classic espresso drinks, iced and blended beverages, energy drinks, teas, lemonades, and seasonal specials. Customers also enjoy the flexibility to customize sweetness levels, milk choices, and flavor combinations.

Coffee & Espresso Drinks

Coffee lovers can choose from a variety of espresso-based drinks such as lattes, americanos, mochas, and cold brews. Dutch Bros uses a proprietary espresso blend that delivers a smooth and bold flavor profile. Drinks are available hot, iced, or blended, depending on preference.

Dutch Bros Rebel Energy Drinks

One of the most iconic offerings is the Rebel energy drink line. Rebels can be customized with dozens of syrups, fruit flavors, and toppings. Customers can choose iced or blended versions, making them especially popular during warmer months.

Teas, Lemonades & Non-Coffee Options

For non-coffee drinkers, the menu includes green tea, black tea, herbal infusions, and refreshing lemonades. These drinks can also be flavored and served hot or iced, offering plenty of caffeine-free or low-caffeine choices.

Seasonal & Secret Menu Items

Dutch Bros frequently introduces limited-time seasonal drinks, featuring unique flavor combinations inspired by holidays and customer favorites. In addition, many fans enjoy ordering from the unofficial “secret menu,” which includes creative drinks shared by the Dutch Bros community.

Customization & Nutrition Information

A major strength of Dutch Bros is customization. Customers can adjust sweetness levels, choose dairy-free milk alternatives, add extra espresso shots, or request sugar-free syrups. Nutrition information varies based on customizations, making it easy to tailor drinks to individual dietary needs.
